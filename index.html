Renewables

LSI Engineering Consultants Limited is a trusted advisor in the renewable energy sector. Our expertise ensures that renewable energy projects are not only sustainable but also optimized for maximum efficiency and long-term success.

Services We Provide

Project Management PMC encompasses engineering review, procurement services, and construction monitoring.

Technical consulting and DPR services include feasibility studies, energy yield assessment, and detailed project report preparation.

Comprehensive evaluation including data validation, design review, O&M practices, energy yield assessment, health tests, and a final evaluation report.

Management of tendering and bidding, including solar function briefings, RFQ preparation, bid evaluation, analysis, and contract finalization.

Oversight services covering design review, project planning, quality assurance, timeline and cost management, and performance testing.

In-depth study of technical feasibility, operational plans, project location suitability, cost benchmarking, generation/PLF vetting, and construction schedule review.

Services include conceptual design review, construction and execution monitoring, quality and safety oversight, and cost and timeline management.
